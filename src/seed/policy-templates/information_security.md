## 1. Purpose

This Information Security Policy establishes the framework for managing information security within {{organisation_name}}. It demonstrates management commitment to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all information assets within the defined scope.

## 2. Scope

{{scope}}

This policy applies to all personnel (employees, contractors, consultants, and temporary staff) and all information assets (data, systems, applications, networks, and physical assets) within the above scope.

## 3. Information Security Objectives

{{organisation_name}} is committed to:

- Protecting information assets against all internal and external threats, whether deliberate or accidental
- Ensuring the confidentiality of information by preventing unauthorised access
- Maintaining the integrity of information by preventing unauthorised modification
- Maintaining the availability of information and systems for authorised users
- Complying with all applicable legal, regulatory and contractual requirements
- Implementing a framework of continual improvement for the ISMS

## 4. Responsibilities

**Top Management** shall:
- Demonstrate visible commitment to information security
- Ensure adequate resources are allocated to the ISMS
- Participate in management reviews of the ISMS

**{{owner}}** shall:
- Maintain and review this policy at least annually
- Ensure policies and procedures are communicated and implemented
- Report on ISMS performance to top management

**All Personnel** shall:
- Read, understand and comply with this policy and supporting policies
- Report actual or suspected information security incidents immediately
- Complete required security awareness training
- Protect assets and access credentials under their control

## 5. Information Security Framework

{{organisation_name}} implements and maintains an Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022. The ISMS includes:

- **Risk Management**: Regular assessment and treatment of information security risks
- **Controls**: Implementation of appropriate Annex A controls based on risk assessment
- **Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring of security controls and ISMS effectiveness
- **Audit**: Regular internal audits and management reviews
- **Improvement**: Corrective actions and continual improvement activities

## 6. Supporting Policies

This policy is supported by topic-specific policies including, but not limited to:
- Access Control Policy
- Risk Management Policy
- Asset Management Policy
- Incident Response Policy
- Business Continuity Policy
- Supplier Security Policy
- Cryptography Policy
- Physical Security Policy
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Data Classification Policy

## 7. Compliance

Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract. Suspected breaches of this policy must be reported immediately to {{owner}}.

## 8. Review

This policy shall be reviewed at least annually or following significant changes to the organisation, its context, or applicable legal and regulatory requirements.
